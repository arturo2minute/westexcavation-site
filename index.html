Services

Specialty excavation and land work that solves rugged property challenges.

West Excavation LLC focuses on the kind of projects that improve access, usability, safety, and long-term function. Whether the work is structural, cleanup-related, or tied to land improvement, each service is built around practical results.

01

Boulder retaining walls and decorative boulders

Installations that bring both structure and character to a property, whether the goal is slope support, visual definition, or a stronger finished look around a driveway or yard.

02

Demolition

Tear-out and site cleanup for damaged structures, unwanted improvements, and spaces that need to be safely cleared before the next phase of work can begin.

03

Brush and land clearing

Clearing overgrowth, reclaiming usable ground, and improving access on properties that need a cleaner starting point for maintenance, construction, or general use.

04

Pond dredging, building, and maintenance

Pond work for owners who need better function, restored capacity, or a cleaner, better-maintained water feature on their land.
